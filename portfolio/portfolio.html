Favorite Games

These aren't just games to me — they're design references, storytelling masterclasses, and endless sources of inspiration.

Sandbox • 2011

Minecraft

Minecraft taught me that constraints breed creativity. A world built from blocks proved that simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.

Metroidvania • 2017

Hollow Knight

A masterclass in atmosphere and player guidance. Team Cherry proved that a small team can create a world more compelling than most AAA titles.

Adventure • 2023

Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

The ultrahand system is a revolution in emergent gameplay. Nintendo's approach to creative problem-solving is something every UI designer should study.

Platformer • 2018

Celeste

Pixel-perfect controls and a narrative that hits hard. The best example of "difficulty as a language" in game design. Also an incredible soundtrack.

RPG • 2019

Disco Elysium

Writing as gameplay. Every dialogue choice reshapes your character's entire psychology. It proved that games can be literature.

Roguelike • 2020

Hades

The gold standard for roguelike progression. Supergiant wove narrative into repetition — every run feels both fresh and meaningful.
